eMasters in Computer Science with specialization in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

1 Batch | The eMasters Certificate in Computer Science with a specialization in AI and Machine Learning is tailor-made for working professionals seeking to elevate their expertise while managing their professional commitments. This flexible two-year program offers a comprehensive curriculum developed by experts from IIIT Hyderabad.

Foundations of Machine Learning (iHuB)

This course provides a solid foundation in machine learning principles and their modern-day applications, aimed at professionals looking to enhance their technical expertise.

AI and ML (PG Level Certificate Programme)

This PG-level certificate programme focuses on AI and Machine Learning, offering deep insights and practical knowledge for professionals aspiring to excel in these fields.

Blockchain and Distributed Ledger Technologies

A comprehensive course covering Blockchain technology and its applications, providing professionals with the knowledge to develop and implement blockchain solutions.

Machine Learning for Chemistry and Drug Design

This course integrates the principles of machine learning with the specialized needs of chemistry and drug design, offering new ways to approach chemical processes and pharmaceutical research.

AI for Medical Professionals

This program is aimed at medical professionals, providing them with the tools and knowledge to leverage AI in healthcare settings.
