Welcome to the Division of Flexible Learning

Prof. Venkatesh Choppella

Associate Dean, Division of Flexible Learning

venkatesh.choppella@iiit.ac.in

Prof. Venkatesh Choppella is a highly respected academic leader and innovator, currently serving as the Associate Dean of the Division of Flexible Learning at IIIT Hyderabad. With a robust background in online education and digital learning strategies, he is dedicated to transforming how education is delivered and experienced in the 21st century.

Prof. Choppella's vision for the DFL is centered around creating a cutting-edge online learning environment that is flexible, engaging, and accessible to a diverse range of learners. He recognizes the importance of adapting to the evolving educational landscape and employs a student-centric approach to develop programs that meet the needs of today’s learners. His initiatives aim to leverage technology to facilitate interactive and personalized learning experiences, bridging the gap between traditional educational models and modern, digital platforms.

A significant aspect of his work includes the development and implementation of Virtual Labs, which provide students with the opportunity to engage in hands-on experiments and simulations in a virtual environment. These labs are designed to enhance the practical learning experience, allowing students to apply theoretical knowledge in real-world scenarios without the constraints of physical lab space. By integrating Virtual Labs into the curriculum, Prof. Choppella ensures that online learners have access to essential practical skills and experiences that are critical for their academic and professional growth.

Under his leadership, the DFL is committed to enhancing the quality of online education through innovative curriculum design, comprehensive support services, and the integration of advanced learning technologies. Prof. Choppella actively promotes the use of multimedia content, adaptive learning systems, and collaborative tools that foster student engagement and participation, ensuring that online learners receive an education that is both rigorous and relevant. In addition to his role in shaping the educational strategies of DFL, he emphasizes the importance of data-driven decision-making to monitor student performance and continuously improve program effectiveness. His commitment to research in online pedagogy and learning analytics enables the DFL to refine its offerings based on insights gained from student interactions and outcomes.
